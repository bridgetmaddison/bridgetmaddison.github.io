You may feel like you are just existing, caught up in self defeating behaviours or avoidance, struggling to get out of your head and on with your life. I believe I can help.

More about me
I am an experienced and Accredited (BABCP) Cognitive Behavioural Psychotherapist and a Registered Mental Health Nurse. I have been privileged to work with many people, empowering them to gain the skills and strategies required to work through their problems. I specialise in Third Wave CBT, particularly Acceptance and Commitment Therapy - a form of CBT that uses Values, Self-Compassion and Mindfulness skills to build a better life. I also work within the Compassion Focused Therapy Model.

I hope you will take the time to have a look through my website, to find out more about Cognitive Behavioural Therapy and me. If you feel this approach might be for you, please contact me and take that first step towards us working together to get your life back on track.
